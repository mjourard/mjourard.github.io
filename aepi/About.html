What is Alpha Epsilon Pi?

Alpha Epsilon Pi provides an opportunity for a Jewish man to be able to join a Jewish organization whose purpose is not specifically religious, but rather social and cultural in nature. Alpha Epsilon Pi is a Jewish fraternity which focuses on developing young leaders while instilling a sense of brotherhood. It provides a home away from home while nurturing the core values that make a mensch, a mensch. It also serves as a link between generations, providing opportunities for young men to join the skilled work force.

Why did I join?

In the final months of high school, my mind wasn't in a great place. I had just broken up with my first long-term girlfriend, I couldn't keep my part-time job because I was moving cities, the youth group I was a part of (NFTY-NEL) is for high schoolers and youth soccer was over for me. It felt like I was losing big parts of my life all at once, and I felt empty.

I was contacted by a two members of the Guelph chapter of AEPi. They gave me their shpiel of what AEPi is and since I was looking for a new group of friends as well as a new community to be a part of, the choice to rush was obvious.

Five years later, I'm an active member of the chapter, keeping in contact with Alumni while providing insight into chapter affairs for the younger leaders.

What do I do in Alpha Epsilon Pi?

I've held a few leadership positions during my tenure here. I was on the executive board as Sentinel in my first year. I was on the Rush committee in my third year. In fourth year, I helped plan our Bar Mitzvah celebration party. At that same party, I won the "Most Improved Brother" award, for my dramatically increased presence that year.

In my fifth year, I've elected to take a step back from leadership to focus on school. That being said, I'm still making an effort to attend every event I can, while offering words of wisdom and encouragement where it's needed.

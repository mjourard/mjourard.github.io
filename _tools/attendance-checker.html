Attendance Checker

This tool compares a list of current attendees on a facebook event against a google sheet that keeps track of appointed "leaders" that are responsible for formally inviting people to the event
  • infoExplanation
    To get the list of facebook attendees:
    1. Go to the event page and click "XY Going * YZ Interested"
    2. Click the going tab
    3. Click and drag while scrolling down until all guests are high lighted
    4. Copy the attendees by pressing ctrl + c|command + c
    You can then paste the attendees into the textbox below
    To get the list of google sheets attendees:
    1. Go to the sheet in Google Sheets
    2. Click File
    3. Click Download -> Comma-separated values
    4. Upload the file here in the Google Sheets section
    5. Alternatively, you can copy + paste the contents of the sheet into the Google Sheets text area
